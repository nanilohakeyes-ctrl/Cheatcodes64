WHAT'S YOUR COLOR SEASON?

Answer 5 quick questions for a fun peek at your color season. There are actually 12–16 seasons in a full analysis — we'll go over your real results in person!

QUESTION 1 OF 5
Look at your inner wrist in natural light. Your veins appear...
This is one of the most reliable ways to read your undertone.
How would you describe the overall depth of your natural coloring?
Think about your skin, hair, and eyes together as one picture.
When you look at your skin up close, your complexion seems...
This helps identify your chroma — how clear or muted your natural coloring is.
What is the contrast between your skin tone and your natural hair/brows?
Stand in front of a mirror and look at how your hair meets your face.
When you wear colors close to your face, which reaction do people give you most?
Think about compliments you've gotten — or times a color just felt wrong.
✦ This is a fun starting point — not a full diagnosis!
